FAQs

How do I see your inventory online?

You can view our inventory from the Order page located on the navigation bar. You can also use that section to track any orders you have being delivered.

My order hasn't arrived yet, what should I do?

You can track your order using the order lookup form on the Order page. You can also use the form located on the Contact Us page to ask us any questions you may have. Our phone number and email address is located on that page as well.

Do you buy items?

Yes! Do you have used books, games, movies, or other cool collectables? Bring them in. We'll take a look at them and see about buying them.
