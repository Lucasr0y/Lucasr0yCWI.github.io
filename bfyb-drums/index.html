About us

We are a locally owned percussion store that has been operating since 2005. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced player we're here to help you find the right equipment to fit your drumming needs. We have everything from pre-built kits to hardware for customizing your own kits to fit your playing style. Feel free to browse through our online catolog or come in and speak to one of our knowledgeable staff members to get you started!
