About Me

Hi there!

Welcome to my home page. Here is where I would like to take a little time to talk about myself and my work ethic. After graduating from Borah High School in Boise, Idaho in 2013 I went straight into my first job working in the food industry at a local horse track. From there I have been working at different resteraunts, most notibly a major pizza chain where I started as a delivery driver in the fall of 2014, until I became an assistant manager three years later and contiued to be one for another three. I enjoyed being an assitant full time but always felt like nothing was ever going to become of that job.

In 2020 I decided that I was going to futher my career opportunities by returning to college. While still continuing to work as an assistant manager full time I began the Software Development program at the College of Western Idaho as a full time student. There I discovered a love for programming. I found I enjoyed putting in the hard work and hours it took to complete programs. While some days proved to be stressful there was no better feeling in seeing a completed program written fully by myself come to life. Each day I expanded my knowledge of programming as well as solidified my time managment skills in order to complete the degree in two years while still continuing my full time work position. I still continue to grow in my knowledge in computer languages and am eager to learn more tools in this ever evolving field.
